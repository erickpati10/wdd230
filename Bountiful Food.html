Location: Santa Monica is situated on the southern coast of California, bordered by the Pacific Ocean to the west. It is approximately 16 miles (26 kilometers) west of downtown Los Angeles.
Climate: Santa Monica enjoys a Mediterranean climate characterized by mild, pleasant winters and warm, dry summers. Average temperatures range from around 55°F (13°C) in winter to 75°F (24°C) in summer. The area typically experiences coastal fog, known as the "June Gloom," during the early summer mornings. Cultural Attractions: The city boasts cultural attractions such as the Santa Monica Pier Aquarium, which provides an educational experience about marine life. The Annenberg Community Beach House offers public facilities, including a pool, gallery space, and community events. The Getty Villa, a museum dedicated to ancient art, is located just outside Santa Monica in the Pacific Palisades.
