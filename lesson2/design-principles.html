Repetition

Delta

delta.com

Delta is a layout for each page that follows the a similar structure with a header, main content area, and footer. Further reinforcing the sense of repetition and consistency.

Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Apple uses a high contrast color scheme with bold white text on a black background, and large images. The layout enhances readability and highlights key information. Additionally, they use different font sizes and styles. It makes it easier to navigate the page and find what they are looking for.

White Space and Clean Design

Squarespace

squarespace.com

The page features a minimalistic and clean design with lots of breathing room between elements, allowing the content to stand out and be easily digestible for the user. they use of a simple color palette, with mainly black and white tones, and a consistent typography style. This design principle helps to create a sense of elegance and sophistication, while also improving the overall user experience by reducing clutter and creating a more visually appealing layout.
